About Us

We started Nivana Earth with a simple idea — "what if your daily bath routine could be good for you and the planet?". That thought led us to create a range of eco-friendly bath products made with clean ingredients and zero plastic. From gentle soaps and solid shampoos to biodegradable toothbrushes, everything we offer is designed to reduce waste without compromising quality. We're not perfect, but we're passionate about making small changes that add up. Thanks for being on this journey with us. Consciously crafted for you and our planet
